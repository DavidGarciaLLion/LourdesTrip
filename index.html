Practical notes

  • Two natural clusters: Argelès-Gazost hub — Spandelles, Soulor, Aubisque & Hautacam all sit within ~2 km of each other; Luz-Saint-Sauveur hub — Luz Ardiden + Tourmalet, ~15–17 km further up the valley.
  • Easiest days from Lourdes: Hautacam, Soulor, Spandelles — all reached via the flat Argelès-Gazost greenway (Voie Verte des Gaves, ~14–16 km each way).
  • Biggest days: Tourmalet and Luz Ardiden — budget ~2.5 h of valley riding round-trip on top of the climb itself.
  • Just outside the radius: Col d'Aspin — its base at Sainte-Marie-de-Campan is reached over the Tourmalet, or ~60 km+ via Arreau.
  • The transfer back is mostly downhill into Lourdes, so the return leg will beat 25 km/h.
